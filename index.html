Frequently Asked Questions

Are you a new company? Can I trust you?
+
Yes — we're newly launched. But that's actually a reason to trust us more: we're personally invested in doing excellent work to build our reputation. Every project is handled by senior experts, not junior staff, and we sign an NDA before any work begins.
How much does a project cost?
+
It depends on scope and complexity. We offer hourly rates for small consulting tasks, and fixed-fee packages for defined projects. We always provide a clear, itemized quote after a free scoping call — no surprises.
How long does a typical project take?
+
A focused analytics report or dashboard can be delivered in 1–3 weeks. More complex ML or AI projects typically run 4–12 weeks. We agree on a timeline during scoping and keep you updated throughout.
How do you protect my data?
+
We sign a mutual NDA before any data is shared. All data is handled with strict access controls, encrypted transfers, and we adhere to GDPR and HIPAA best practices where applicable. Your data is never shared with third parties.
Do you work with small businesses and startups?
+
Absolutely — in fact, that's our sweet spot right now. We have flexible engagement models designed to fit startup budgets, and we love working with organizations at the early stage of building their data capabilities.
What does the free consultation include?
+
A free 30-minute call where we listen to your challenge, assess what data work is needed, and share an honest view of what we can do and how long it will take. No pitch, no pressure — just a real conversation.
